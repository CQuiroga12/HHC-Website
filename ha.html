Spring Break Trip to Manchester

Home Chris

During my Spring Break in 2019, I went to Manchester, UK. Here are some of my favorite photos and quotes from the trip:

Philadelphia Museum of Art

This picture reminds me of our group’s one-day delay in Philadelphia before arriving in Manchester. Although the delay was unpleasant, it was still a nice experience to explore Philadelphia.

Manchester street and modern buildings

This street is full of modern and beautiful buildings, which shows the great, present-day landscape of Manchester.

The “Baby”, the first computer to store and run the program – Science and Industry Museum, Manchester, UK

This machine illustrates the amazing growth of the scientific, industrial, and technological scene of Manchester. It is also interesting to see the start of the digital evolution, without which we could never had access to the great pool of information like today.

Eleanor Rigby Statue – Liverpool, UK

This statue is based on the subject of the The Beatles’ song “Eleanor Rigby”. There was actually a headstone with the name Eleanor Rigby in a church fete where John Lennon and Paul McCartney first met.
